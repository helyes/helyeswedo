FAQ

  • What should I wear?

    The dress code at our wedding is formal wear or black tie optional.

  • How should I get there?
    From Gold Coast

    The closest airport to the wedding venue is Gold Coast Airport. It is about 40 - 50 mins drive from the airport to the venue depending on traffic. There are plenty of Ubers, and taxis that would be around the area.

    From Brisbane

    There are direct trains from Brisbane airport to Gold Coast. The other option is to drive down from Brisbane which is about an hour to an hour and a half from the city.

  • Where should I stay?

    We will be staying at the Sheraton Grand Mirage Resort. Main Beach is the area we recommend our guests to stay as it's close to our wedding venue. There are a lot of holiday apartments, hotels, airbnb to suit all budgets. We have a few accomodations that are close by listed above which should be within 10 - 15 mins to the venue.
