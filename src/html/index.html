FAQ

  • What should I wear?

    The dress code at our wedding is formal wear or black tie optional.

  • How should I get there?

    The closest airport to the wedding venue is Gold Coast Airport. It is about 40 - 50 mins drive from the airport to the venue depending on traffic. There are plenty of Ubers, and taxis that would be around the area.

  • Where should I stay?

    We will be staying at the Sheraton Grand Mirage Resort. Main Beach is the area we recommend our guests to stay as it's close to our wedding venue. There are a lot of holiday apartments, hotels, airbnb to suit all budgets. We have a few accomodations that are close by listed above which should be about 10 - 15 mins drive to the venue.

  • What's there to do in Gold Coast?

    There are lots of things to do in Gold Coast! We suggest going to the beach, and do some water activities! Pacific Fair Shopping Centre is a huge shopping center for all my shopaholic friends. Surfer's Paradise is a must! If you have a car, drive up to Hope Island and Sanctuary Cove - it's beautiful there! We suggest going there for a relaxing lunch! For golf lovers like my dad, there are heaps of golf courses close by! Enjoy the sunshine! Let us know how we can help organise your trip!
